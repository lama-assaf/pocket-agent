# /launch

build a launch plan that gets from "shipped" to "adopted".

## what it does

forces these inputs:

1. who are we trying to reach (specific audience)
2. what behavior do we want from them
3. what's the story (the one-line and the longer version)
4. what does success look like (metrics + timeframe)

then builds:

- timeline (T-4w through T+4w)
- channel selection (owned, earned, partner, paid)
- day-one risk list with owners
- post-launch follow-up plan

## how to use

```
/launch [product or feature name]
```

## output

structured timeline, channel matrix, narrative (short and long forms), day-one risk register, post-launch plan.

## what it doesn't do

- write the entire blog post (that's /landing or /case-study)
- guarantee press (it plans, it doesn't pitch)
