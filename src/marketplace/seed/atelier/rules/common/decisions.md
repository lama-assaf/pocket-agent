# decisions

how decisions get made and recorded.

## name the decision

every decision worth recording has: what's being decided, why now, who decides, what alternatives were considered.

## record the tradeoff

every decision involves tradeoffs. if a decision feels free, you haven't identified the tradeoff yet.

## reversible vs irreversible

if you can change it without significant cost, decide and move on. if you can't, take longer, gather more input, and write the decision down.

## decision logs over meeting notes

a decision log captures: the decision, the date, the people involved, the alternatives considered, the reasoning. it's a record for future you and the team.

## disagree and commit, in writing

when reasonable people disagree, the decider decides, the team commits. record the disagreement in the log so it can be revisited if context changes.
