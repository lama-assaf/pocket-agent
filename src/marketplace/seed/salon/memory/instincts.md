## examples

(delete these once you have your own.)

- **posting windows**: default to the audience's local morning and early-evening windows; never schedule a launch post during a known dead zone (weekends for b2b, weekday mornings for consumer).
- **comment-first discipline**: before drafting new content, triage open replies and mentions older than 2 hours — engagement compounds faster than fresh posts.
- **banned topics**: no political commentary, no competitor callouts by name, no unverified stats. when in doubt, cut the line.
- **voice check before publish**: every draft gets checked against voice.md's adjective sliders before it goes out, not after.
- **never**: never post a thread or announcement without a golden-hour engagement plan already staged.
- **platform-native, not copy-paste**: the same idea gets rewritten per platform's register (thread vs. post vs. announcement vs. broadcast) — never the same text shipped three places.
