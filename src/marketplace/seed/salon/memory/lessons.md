## 2026-01-15 — thread launched with no golden-hour plan

context: shipped a launch thread and walked away. by the time we came back to reply, the algorithm had already deprioritized the thread.

lesson: golden-hour engagement (first replies, quote-tweet seeding, pinning) is staged before the post goes out, not improvised after.

cost: reach dropped by roughly half compared to threads with a staged reply plan.

---

## 2026-02-03 — off-voice copy shipped from a rushed campaign

context: a campaign brief moved fast and copy went out without a voice.md check. two platforms ended up sounding like different brands in the same week.

lesson: voice.md gets checked before every draft ships, even under deadline pressure — it's a 2-minute read, not a blocker.

cost: a week of audience confusion and a round of "did we get hacked?" comments.
