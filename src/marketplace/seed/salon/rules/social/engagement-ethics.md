# Engagement Ethics

Community trust is earned through authentic participation. These are non-negotiable.

## Core Rules

- **No astroturfing or sockpuppets:** Never create fake accounts to amplify your voice or simulate grassroots support
- **No engagement pods or coordinated inauthentic amplification:** Don't join networks designed to artificially boost numbers; it breaks trust and violates most platform ToS
- **Disclose paid or affiliated promotion:** If you're paid, sponsored, or have a financial stake, say so clearly and upfront
- **Comments add value:** Every reply must add something the thread didn't have. Never post bare "great post" or emoji-only engagement

## Web3 & Financial Contexts

- **No price talk that reads as financial advice:** Discussing price movements requires extreme care—assume regulators are reading
- **No shilling claims:** Avoid language that resembles unregistered-security promotion ("this token will moon," "guaranteed returns," etc.)

## Community Participation

- **Respect raid etiquette:** If you're amplifying from one community to another, arrive with substance, not a brigade
- **No drive-by self-promotion:** Joining a community solely to push your thing reads as inauthentic and burns bridges
