# banned words and patterns

words that almost always weaken writing. ban or use with extreme care.

## generic ai tone

- delve / delving / delve into
- navigate the complexities
- in today's fast-paced world
- leverage / leveraging
- robust / robustness
- seamless / seamlessly
- elevate / elevating
- empower / empowering
- harness / harnessing
- tapestry / rich tapestry
- realm / realm of
- foster / fostering
- meticulous / meticulously
- cutting-edge
- state-of-the-art
- groundbreaking
- revolutionary
- game-changing
- unparalleled

## corporate filler

- circle back
- touch base
- synergies / synergistic
- at the end of the day
- bandwidth (used figuratively)
- low-hanging fruit
- move the needle
- value-add
- best-in-class
- world-class
- best-of-breed

## empty intensifiers

- truly
- really
- simply
- just (often)
- very
- absolutely
- literally (when used non-literally)

## hollow openers

- "we listened to your feedback"
- "exciting news"
- "we're thrilled to announce"
- "in today's [adjective] [noun]"
- "welcome to the future of"

## the test

before publishing, search for every word in this list. for each hit, either rewrite the sentence or justify the use.

## exceptions

some words on this list have legitimate uses. "leverage" as a financial term is fine. "robust" describing a technical system that has been load-tested is sometimes earned. the ban is against unconsidered use.
